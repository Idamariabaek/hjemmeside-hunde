Where should I adopt from?

Finding a reputable breeder or adopting from a responsible animal shelter are two common ways to acquire a healthy puppy.

Reputable breeders prioritize the health and well-being of their puppies, ensuring proper medical care and socialization.

Animal shelters often have puppies in need of loving homes, and adopting from them not only saves a life but also promotes responsible pet ownership.
